gavel About
NEMUN 2023
NEMUN is an annual Model United Nations conference held by the NEHS MUN club. NEHS has successfully hosted NEMUN, once named EMUN, from 2013-2022. This year held on May 20 - 21, NEMUN is a brand new start with a bigger dream.
format_quote Be a voice,
not an echo.
We are aiming for an innovative and high-quality conference with talented students from all across the island coming together and contributing to our society with inquisitive minds. We welcome you to join the conference, and look forward to seeing you at NEMUN 2023!
